# 🏀 Basketball Player Performance Analysis (Power BI)

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes a professional basketball player's shooting performance using historical shot-level data (26,000+ shots).

The goal is to uncover efficiency trends, shot selection patterns, and performance variations across different conditions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall shooting efficiency (FG%)
- Evaluate performance trends across seasons
- Assess shot selection effectiveness
- Compare performance by venue and opponent

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1. Executive Summary
- KPIs: Total Shots, Shots Made, FG%
- Trend analysis: Volume vs Efficiency
- Interactive filters (Season, Shot Type)

### 2. Shot Analysis
- Shot distribution by zone
- Efficiency by shot type
- Home vs Away comparison

### 3. Opponent Analysis
- Performance variation by opponent
- Identification of strong and weak matchups

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Heavy reliance on mid-range shots reduces efficiency ceiling
- FG% declines during high shot-volume seasons
- Performance varies significantly by opponent
- Highest efficiency: Dunk zone
- Lowest efficiency: Tip shot zone

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
- Optimize shot selection toward high-value zones
- Balance volume with efficiency
- Apply matchup-based scoring strategies
